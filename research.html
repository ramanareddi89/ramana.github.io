About / Teaching / Research / CV

Topological Data Analysis (TDA) is a subfield of algebraic topology whose applications in fields beyond mathematics are becoming increasingly common. In computer science, particularly in machine learning and networks, TDA has been used to incorporate the topology of input data to inform models and embed topological information directly into model design. TDA helps us extract topological features from data, which, when combined with statistical tools, can provide insights into the data's structure and significance. My research focuses on answering structural questions about how artificial neural networks function through the study of these topological features and their statistical relevance.
